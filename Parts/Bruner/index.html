Spare Parts for (obsolete) Bruner HBR

Spare Parts for (obsolete) Bruner Water Softeners are available through Culligan of Danville Kentucky (located in Central KY) for Commercial and Industrial end use custmoers in North America. The Bruner Water Softeners utilize a multi-port valve system and can be retrofitted with GBE or MVP controllers to replace the original Bruner IQS or IQS/jr systems. Quotations/Cost Estimates are available for (obsolete) Bruner water softener parts, including replacement D-180 multiport valve, Culligan GBE and MVP electronic controllers as well as the Culligan CSM softerner as a replacement for complete Bruner Water Softener systems for capital projects. The Complete Bruner Water Softener systems were last sold in 1996. The parts below are also applicable to Cleaver-Brooks water Softeners.
