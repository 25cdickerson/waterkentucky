Spare Parts for Culligan HE High Efficiency 1.5" Softener with Smart Controller

Spare Parts for Culligan High Efficiency HE1.5 single and HET 1.5 duplex systems are available through Culligan of Danville Kentucky (located in Central KY) for HE 1.5 Water Softeners for Commercial and Industrial end use customers in North America. The Culligan HE 1.5 single softener and Culligan HET 1.5 duplex softeners have GBE electronic controllers. Quotations/Cost Estimates are available for Culligan HE 1.5 and HET 1.5 duplex water softener parts as well as for complete units for capital projects.
