Spare Parts for Culligan HI-FLO 3e

Spare Parts for the Culligan HI-FLO 3e system are available through Culligan of Danville Kentucky (located in Central KY) for HI-FLO 3e Water Softeners for Commercial and Industrial end use customers in North America. The HI-FLO 3e softener has a GBE electronic controller timer system with Fleck 2" (Fleck 2900) and 3" (Fleck 3900) valves. Quotations/Cost Estimates are available for Culligan HI-FLO 3e water softener parts as well as complete units for capital projects.
