Spare Parts for Culligan HI-FLO 3

Spare Parts for the Culligan HI-FLO 3 system are available through Culligan of Danville Kentucky (located in Central KY) for HI-FLO 3 Water Softeners for Commercial and Industrial end use customers in North America. The HI-FLO 3 softener has an electromechanical timer system with a Fleck 2850 1.5" valve as well as a 2" valve (Fleck 2900) system. Quotations/Cost Estimates are available for Culligan HI-FLO 3 water softener parts as well as complete units for capital projects.
