Spare Parts for Culligan (obsolete) HI-FLO 55 / HI-FLO 55e

Spare Parts for (obsolete) HI-FLO 55 and HI-FLO 55e Water Softeners are available through Culligan of Danville Kentucky (located in Central KY) for Commercial and Industrial end use customers in North America. The (obsolete) HI-FLO 55 Water Softeners utilize a proprietary valve system with six ports that house cartridges. The valve is no longer available, although replacement cartridges are. The HI-FLO 55 mechanical timer/controllers are no longer available. However, they can be replaced with the GBE or MVP electronic controllers. The electronic timer utilized by the Culligan HI-FLO55e softeners are obsolete, but can also be replaced with the Culligan MVP or GBE controllers. Quotations / Cost Estimates are available for replacement water softener parts.
