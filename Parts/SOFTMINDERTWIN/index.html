Spare Parts for Culligan Soft-Minder Twin

Spare Parts for (obsolete) Culligan Soft-Minder Twin Water Softeners are available through Culligan of Danville Kentucky (located in Central KY) for Commercial and Industrial end use customers in North America. The Culligan SMT water softeners utilize a Fleck 9000 or 9100 valve for the 1" system and a Fleck 9500 valve for the 1.5" systems. Quotations / Cost Estimates are available for replacement water softener parts.
